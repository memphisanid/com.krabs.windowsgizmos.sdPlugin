Description of Window Modes

 Normal - Window will display normally (Not minimized or maximized).

 Minimize - Window will minimize.

 Maximize - Window will maximize.

 * Hide - Window will be hidden (It will no longer be able to be seen).

 * Show - Window will be shown (This reverses the Hide action).

 * Force Minimize - Window will be forced to minimize.

 * Force Maximize - Window will be forced to maximize.

 (Use * modes at your own risk and only if you understand the possible consequences.)
